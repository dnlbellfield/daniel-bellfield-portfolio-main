About

Web and email developer focused on digital campaign production.

I’m Daniel, a web and email developer focused on digital campaign production. I’ve always been drawn to the production side of creative work: building things, working with a team, solving problems, and learning from what happens after the work goes out. Today, I bring that mindset to emails, web pages, campaign setup, QA, and small tools that help digital work get built, checked, and ready.

I’m comfortable in the middle of a project, where creative direction, technical requirements, platform limits, and timelines have to line up. I help clarify details, solve production issues, and keep the work moving.

Since 2022, I've worked on responsive HTML emails, Salesforce Marketing Cloud email production, CMS updates, and workflow-focused web tools. Outside of work, I build projects like EmailClarity and Portfolio-Up to keep learning, test new ideas, and explore better ways to support digital production.
