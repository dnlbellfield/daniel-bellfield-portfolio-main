About

Web and email developer.

Hi, I'm Daniel — a web and email developer based in California.

Since 2022, I've shipped email builds, landing pages, CMS updates, QA fixes, platform setup, and the small details that keep work moving.

The part I actually like is the practical stuff — learning how a system works, finding the pattern someone already built, and making the next round easier than the last.

I use AI with context, review, and judgment. The value is knowing the campaign problem well enough to guide the tool toward something useful.

Outside of work, I build live projects like EmailClarity and Portfolio-Up to keep sharpening my thinking around email, analytics, web development, and internal tools.
