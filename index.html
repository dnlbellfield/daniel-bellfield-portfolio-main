About

Practical support, calmly delivered.

Development help for the middle of digital production — where marketing goals, platforms, and implementation details meet.

Hi, I'm Daniel — a web and email developer based in California.

Since 2022, I've worked on the production side of digital campaigns: emails, landing pages, CMS updates, QA, platform setup, and the small fixes that keep launches moving.

I like the practical part of development: learning the system, finding the existing pattern, asking the useful question, building cleanly, testing the details, and leaving the next version easier to handle.

I use AI the same way I use any production tool: with context, review, and a real understanding of the work. The value is not asking AI to guess the job; it is knowing the campaign problem well enough to guide the tool toward something useful.

Outside of work, I build projects like EmailClarity and Portfolio-Up to keep sharpening my thinking around email, analytics, web development, and internal tools.
